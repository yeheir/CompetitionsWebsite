{% include "flash.html" %} Taekwondo ITF is a Korean martial art founded by General Choi Hong Hi in 1955. It focuses on self-defense techniques, patterns called "Tuls," and sparring. "Tuls" are choreographed movements that help students practice various techniques. Sparring can be non-contact or full-contact, depending on skill level.

The martial art places importance on traditional values and discipline. ITF Taekwondo also includes a promotion and grading system, where students advance through colored belts, reflecting their skill development and proficiency. This martial art not only enhances physical fitness but also instills mental discipline, respect, and self-confidence in practitioners.
